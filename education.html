My Educational Journey in Moses Lake, Washington

Being a Mechanical Technician at Group 14 Technologies means my education has to be practical – operations, maintenance, leading people. Started with the basics at Big Bend, moved into business at WSU, then got the hands-on maritime side from MAMA. All of it helps me do the job right every day, and it ties right into my faith at Grace Harvest Church and being a dad. You can see how it connects to my community work.

Education's more than just the degrees you end up with – it's the stuff that sticks with you. All of it works together to help me handle the job, mentor kids, and be a better dad right here in Moses Lake. Faith keeps it all grounded.
