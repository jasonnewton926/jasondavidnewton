Professional Background

I earned my Bachelor of Arts in Business Management & Operations from Washington State University in 2016 and hold a U.S. Coast Guard Merchant Mariner Credential (QMED). My maritime and industrial experience includes operating refrigeration plants, diesel-electric power systems, and large-scale industrial production lines.

At Simplot FG, I supervised teams of 30–50 personnel, ensuring plant operations, maintenance, and safety standards were exceeded. I have a proven track record in leadership, operational excellence, and process optimization.
