About Jason

Jason David Newton is a mechanical and operations professional based in Moses Lake, Washington. With extensive experience in industrial manufacturing, maritime engineering, and operational leadership, he has built a career focused on problem solving, technical excellence, and team leadership.

Jason currently works as a Mechanical Technician at Group 14 Technologies and previously served as a Plant Shift Manager and Process Maintenance Supervisor at Simplot FG.

Beyond his professional work, Jason is a father, a man of faith, and an active member of his local community. His interests include strength training, mentoring youth, studying scripture, and personal development.
