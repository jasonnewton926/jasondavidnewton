Jason David Newton

Moses Lake, Washington

Mechanical Technician • Operations Leader • Father • Man of Faith

Welcome to my personal website. I’m a Washington-based mechanical and operations professional with a background in industrial manufacturing, maritime engineering, and team leadership. Over the past decade I’ve worked in demanding technical environments ranging from large-scale food production facilities to commercial fishing vessels operating complex mechanical systems at sea.

Today I work as a Mechanical Technician at Group14 Technologies in Moses Lake, where I focus on maintenance, fabrication, and ensuring reliability in high-performance industrial systems.

Faith, Family, and Personal Growth

Outside of work, the most important roles in my life are being a father and continuing to grow in my faith in Jesus Christ. Fatherhood has been the greatest blessing of my life and a responsibility I take seriously every day.

My faith plays a central role in how I approach life, relationships, and work. I am an active member of Grace Harvest Church in Moses Lake where I continue to grow spiritually and serve within the church community.

I believe that living faithfully, loving people well, and serving others with humility are some of the most meaningful ways we can impact the world around us.

Professional Background

I earned my Bachelor of Arts in Business Management & Operations from Washington State University in 2016. My career has since taken me through multiple industries including manufacturing, heavy industrial maintenance, and maritime engineering.

I also hold a U.S. Coast Guard Merchant Mariner Credential as a Qualified Member of the Engineering Department (QMED), where I operated and maintained large-scale refrigeration plants, diesel-electric power systems, and onboard processing equipment while working in the commercial fishing industry.

In leadership roles at Simplot FG I supervised teams of 30–50 personnel, managing plant operations, maintenance coordination, safety, and production performance.
