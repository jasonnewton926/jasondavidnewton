Professional Background of Jason David Newton

Group 14 Technologies · Simplot FG · USCG Credentialed

I graduated from Washington State University with a Bachelor of Arts in Business Management & Operations, and hold a U.S. Coast Guard Merchant Mariner Credential. See details on my education page. My experience spans operating refrigeration systems, diesel-electric power, and industrial lines.

At Simplot FG, I led teams of 30–50, focusing on operations, safety, and efficiency. Now at Group 14, I handle maintenance and planning. It's rewarding work that fits my life here in Moses Lake.
