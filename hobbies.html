My Hobbies & Adventures in Moses Lake, Washington

Outside of work at Group 14 Technologies (see my experience) and family/community commitments (explore my community page), I recharge through a mix of physical activities, entertainment, and enjoying the Pacific Northwest. These hobbies keep me balanced, energized, and connected to the beauty around Moses Lake, WA. Reflections in my blog.

My Favorite Hobbies

Lifting Weights

Strength training is a daily ritual—building discipline and resilience that carries over to everything else. Whether at home or the gym, it's my go-to for staying strong and focused in Moses Lake.

Golf

Love hitting the links around Grant County. It's relaxing, challenging, and a great way to enjoy Washington's open spaces. Looking for local course recommendations? Hit me up!

Snowboarding

When winter hits the Pacific Northwest, I'm on the slopes. The thrill of carving fresh powder recharges me—perfect escape from the everyday in Moses Lake and beyond.

Music & Movies

Cranking tunes or diving into a good film is my unwind time. From classic rock to new releases, it's all about good vibes and storytelling after a long day.

Reading

Whether leadership books, biographies, or faith-inspired reads, books fuel personal growth and quiet reflection—essential in my busy life. Check related resources.

Pacific Northwest Adventures

Exploring Washington's lakes, trails, and scenery keeps me grounded. From local Moses Lake spots to bigger PNW getaways, the outdoors inspire gratitude and adventure.

Why These Matter

These hobbies aren't just fun—they help me stay sharp, connect with nature, and bring balance amid work and family life. Living in Moses Lake gives the perfect backdrop for it all. Got similar interests or PNW tips? Share them with me or connect on socials!
