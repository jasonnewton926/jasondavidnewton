About Me

I am a results-driven mechanical and operations professional with a strong foundation in business management and hands-on technical expertise in industrial and maritime environments. With over a decade of experience spanning manufacturing, maintenance, and engineering, I thrive at the intersection of leadership, process optimization, and hands-on problem-solving.

Currently, I serve as a Mechanical Technician at Group 14 Technologies, managing maintenance, repairs, and fabrication projects while stepping into planning and scheduling responsibilities. Previously, I led teams of 30–50 personnel at Simplot FG, overseeing plant operations, process efficiency, and maintenance initiatives, and contributed to the successful commissioning of a major production line.

My maritime background as a U.S.C.G. Certified Qualified Member of the Engineering Department (QMED) has equipped me with advanced technical skills in large-scale refrigeration, diesel-electric power plants, and onboard processing systems.

Outside of work, I am a father, a man of faith, and an active member of my community. I volunteer as a youth football coach, serve at Grace Harvest Church, and enjoy strength training, Bible study, and quality time with my son.
