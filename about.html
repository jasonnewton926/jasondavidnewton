About Jason David Newton

Moses Lake, Washington

I’m a results-driven mechanical and operations professional with over a decade of experience spanning industrial manufacturing, heavy maintenance, and maritime engineering. My work is guided by integrity, technical skill, and a commitment to excellence.

Professional Background

I earned my Bachelor of Arts in Business Management & Operations from Washington State University in 2016. My career has been focused on maintaining critical systems, leading teams, and optimizing processes across industrial and maritime environments.

Currently, I serve as a Mechanical Technician at Group 14 Technologies in Moses Lake, managing maintenance, fabrication, and planning efforts to ensure operational reliability and efficiency.

Maritime Engineering Experience

I hold a U.S. Coast Guard Merchant Mariner Credential (QMED) from Mid-Atlantic Maritime Academy. During my time aboard commercial fishing vessels, I operated and maintained complex mechanical systems including large-scale refrigeration plants, boilers, diesel-electric power systems, and onboard fish processing equipment.

This experience strengthened my technical expertise and reinforced the importance of teamwork, discipline, and reliability in high-pressure, mission-critical environments.

Leadership & Community

I’ve led teams of 30–50 personnel at Simplot FG, overseeing safety, production, and maintenance. Leadership for me is about empowering others, driving results, and creating a culture of accountability and growth.

Outside of work, I remain committed to community engagement. I have coached youth football, volunteered locally, and actively participate in my church to serve and mentor others.

Faith, Family & Personal Life

Being a father is my most important role. Raising my son with love, guidance, and example drives everything I do.

My faith in Jesus Christ shapes my decisions, relationships, and work ethic. I aim to live faithfully, serve others, and grow personally and spiritually every day.

In my personal time, I enjoy lifting weights, golf, snowboarding, Bible study, movies, music, reading, and coaching youth sports.
