Community Involvement

Moses Lake, Washington

Giving back to Moses Lake is a big part of who I am. Through faith at Grace Harvest Church, coaching kids in football, and local volunteering, I try to make a difference. It's all tied to my family life and work – keeps me grounded. Check out my personal reflections for more on this.

Key Areas of Involvement

Grace Harvest Church

Active member at Grace Harvest Church in Moses Lake (313 Fig St). I join worship, Bible study, and outreach. Their vision – worship God, serve the city, make disciples – guides what I do. Come Sundays at 9:00am or 11:00am for teaching and community. More at graceharvestchurch.org. See faith tools in resources.

Youth Football Coaching

Coaching kids in Moses Lake through Grid Kids and Columbia Basin programs. Back in 2017-2018, I coached the 7th grade team at Frontier Middle School – those seasons were full of ups and downs, but watching the kids learn teamwork and push themselves was incredible. I teach discipline and perseverance – stuff from my career that helps on and off the field. Seeing them grow is the best part. Photos in my gallery.

Mentoring & Volunteering

Through church and local events, I mentor youth and volunteer in Grant County. It's about supporting families and building community – aligns with my interests in growth and the Pacific Northwest.

If you’ve got a volunteering opportunity or need a hand with something around Moses Lake, feel free to reach out – I’d be happy to help or point you in the right direction.
