Community Involvement in Moses Lake, Washington

Giving back to my community is a core part of who I am. As a resident of Moses Lake, Washington, I’m deeply committed to serving others through faith, mentoring, and local activities. My involvement reflects my Christian values from Grace Harvest Church and my passion for guiding the next generation as a father and coach. Explore how this ties into my personal reflections.

Key Areas of Involvement

  • Grace Harvest Church

    Active member of Grace Harvest Church in Moses Lake, WA (313 Fig St). I participate in worship services, Bible study, and community outreach efforts. The church's vision—"Together we will worship God, serve our city, and make disciples"—guides my service. Join us Sundays at 9:00am or 11:00am for practical Bible teaching and fellowship. Learn more at graceharvestchurch.org. See related faith resources.

  • Youth Football Coaching

    Coaching youth football in Moses Lake through programs like Moses Lake Grid Kids and Columbia Basin Youth Football (e.g., Jr. Mavericks). I mentor young athletes in teamwork, discipline, and resilience—skills that translate from my professional life to the field. It's rewarding to see kids grow, build confidence, and have fun while learning life lessons rooted in hard work and faith. Photos in my gallery.

  • Mentoring & Volunteering

    Through church and community events, I volunteer in mentoring roles, supporting families and youth in Moses Lake. Whether it's helping with local initiatives or encouraging personal growth, I aim to make a positive impact in Grant County and the Pacific Northwest. This aligns with my personal interests in youth development.

Why Community Matters to Me

My faith at Grace Harvest Church teaches that serving others is central to a meaningful life. Balancing my role at Group 14 Technologies with family and community keeps me grounded. These experiences enrich my fatherhood and inspire ongoing growth. Interested in getting involved or chatting about Moses Lake life? Reach out or connect on socials!
